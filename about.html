At Edesia Catering, we always strive to offer quality, locally sourced produce from here in somerset. Edesia Catering is currently ran by a small team, including chefs with over 15 years experience, and other members of staff who are dedicated to helping grow the company and providing great service to our customers.

We have a wide range of menus, and flexibility within the individual menus, we are able to cater for a vast number of different events, from weddings and engagements to birthdays and corporate events across the whole of somerset.

Everyone at Edesia Catering values flexibility and aims to be able to cater for every guest at your event. By providing vegetarian and gluten free options as well as allowing for our customers to adapt their menus, you can be safe in the knowledge that you and all your guests will be satisfied with the food on offer.

Most importantly to our goals moving forward is to make our customers feel safe in the knowledge that the event goes smoothly, and we deliver on our standards for every dish we serve, because your event matters to us.
