SUSTAINABILITY

As our business grows and changes, our desire to use local sustainable produce will remain the same. We want to help support local businesses and farms to thrive, and as such use the fantastic produce on offer here in the south west to achieve this.

Nearly 100% of the vegetables we use are grown here in the south west with the exception of a small variety of more exotic products from further afield or even abroad. However we always aim to use organic produce no matter where it is sourced.
