We have a range of services dedicated to making any event one to remember for you and your guests. See our full range below.

WEDDINGS

Our wedding packages can be tailored to meet your needs. From hog roasts and buffets to 3 course meals, a bar service at hand to take you into the early hours, and a dedicate, talented team that will happily go beyond the call of catering to make your day that little bit more special, we have everything covered for your special day.

We are with you 100% of the way and will work closely with you to ensure your catering experience with us goes the way you imagined. From meticulously creating a menu that fits your desire and tastes, ensuring the plan for the day fits around your needs and using professional and friendly staff to deliver all of this. We will also provide all of your cutlery, crockery, glasses etc at no further cost. as well as being able to catering for outdoor/marquee weddings.

Our bar service can be used for as long as you need, this will come free of charge for wedding parties of more than 70 guests, otherwise the bar can be hired for £150.




CORPORATE EVENTS

We understand business, and the importance of delivering to our customers as well as looking after our staff. We take these values and put it into our services, making sure that your clients and staff are catered to our own standards. Impress that new investor, or make sure your staff are well fed during that long training day. What ever you need, rely on us to make it happen.

We cater for all manners of corporate events. We can supply you with a lunch service for those training days to 3 course sit down meals for your private and public functions. We have many options to choose from, including breakfast, lunches, buffets and sit down meals.

We are able to deliver and provide all necessary amenities to complete your catering experience. we also offer a coffee/tea station for those long training days, or for meeting venues that do not offer this.

HOG ROAST

Our pulled pork hog roast offers great value for money, as the pork is locally sourced and shredded, ready to be served to guests. The pull pork comes in a bun with stuffing, potatoes, salad bar and a choice of apple or chipotle sauce, all without breaking the bank when considering having many of guests attending.

The pulled pork hog roast is a great alternative to evening buffets at weddings and especially great for outdoor events in the summer months when thinking about budget.

DELIVERY & SETUP

Edesia Catering will prep everything for your event and deliver it to any specified venue within somerset, lay out the food and leave you and your guests to it. This is a great service for occasions such as corporate events, surprise birthdays, anniversaries and wakes, where you may not want the intrusion of catering staff during the event.
